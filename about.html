THE NAVIX STORY

The Mekong Delta, Vietnam's agricultural powerhouse, faces a critical logistics challenge: 70-75% of goods rely on costly road transport, inflating costs by up to 30% of product value.

NaviX was founded to transform this narrative. We saw an opportunity in the region's dense river network and emerging infrastructure like Tran De Deep-water Port. By adopting an Asset-Heavy Hybrid model with our own vessels, we provide reliable, cost-optimized waterway solutions that directly address market gaps.

We're not just moving cargo; we're repositioning the entire Mekong Delta as an international logistics hub.
